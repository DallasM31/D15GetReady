Welcome!

Disasters and emergencies can strike at any time. And when they do, you will have little to no time to prepare for the next few days or weeks of an emergency.
And when the rest of the neighborhood is clamoring for the same scant supplies — food, water, heat sources — the last thing you would want is to be empty-handed.

This is where an emergency checklist and a stash of emergency supplies come in handy. We hope to provide you with the information you need for an emergency situation. Our easy-to-follow guides provide a practical disaster preparation plan for both individuals and families. With tips, stories, and checklists, anyone with a preparedness mindset will find it easy to take crucial steps toward developing an emergency preparedness plan.
